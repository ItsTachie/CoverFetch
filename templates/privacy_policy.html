Privacy Policy

CoverFetch uses the Spotify API to access and display your saved album covers.

We value your privacy. This application:

  • Does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.
  • Only requests permission to read your saved albums.
  • Does not send emails or share data with third parties.

Revoking Access

You can revoke this app's access to your Spotify account at any time by visiting your Spotify app settings.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out via Instagram.
